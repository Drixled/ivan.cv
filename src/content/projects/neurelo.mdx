## Product Design

<div class="narrative max-w-2xl mx-auto">

### Setting the foundation

When I joined, the product had a few engineer-built screens and no design direction. I'd never designed for databases before, so I learned the domain from scratch, which helped me spot friction that people closer to the space stopped noticing. Redesigned the onboarding from 12 steps to 4. Shipped the full product redesign in 8 weeks.

</div>

## Design System

<div class="narrative max-w-2xl mx-auto">

### Tokens, theming, and getting buy-in

Built a token-based design system with 60+ components and a theming architecture. Presented the approach to engineering to get buy-in. Dark mode shipped as a theme swap, not a rebuild. What would have taken weeks took less than a day.

</div>
