About Me

Over the past decade, I've designed for early-stage startups across B2C, B2B, and developer tools. Some of that work was for companies backed by Sequoia and Zetta.

I've led end-to-end design, from product to brand, and helped technical products make sense to real people.

Beyond software, I design custom keyboards and keycaps as tactile artifacts—built with care, meant to be felt.
